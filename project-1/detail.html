Alice

Hi I’m Alice. I am a calico (still very much a kitten)! I have already been a mama and an amazing one at that! My foster mom says I am very sweet and super social. I love to play with my toys and the two residents cats. I have not met a dog yet, but I’m very friendly and think I’d like them too! I met some kids the other day and had a blast playing with the toddler. At nap time I like to snuggle up on a big fuzzy blanket and show my foster mom affection by purring on her lap. I’m ready to find my forever home! Alice is litterbox trained, estimated to be one year old and weighs about 8 lbs. All cats have had a vet exam, are up to date on vaccinations, have been given a dewormer and treated for fleas and ear mites if needed. Please note that because we require a home visit at the time of adoption we can only place animals in homes within a 100 mile radius of Minneapolis.
